NEPAL

Nepal, country of Asia, lying along the southern slopes of the Himalayan mountain ranges. It is a landlocked country located between India to the east, south, and west and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China to the north. Its territory extends roughly 500 miles (800 kilometres) from east to west and 90 to 150 miles from north to south. The capital is Kathmandu.

Nepal is a country full of diversity , rich in cultures, traditions , languages, religions , ethics and so on.. Nepal is a beautiful country situated at the foothills of the himalayas and has special gift from nature. It's geographical structure , it's environment has its own uniqueness. From the plain terai to high himalayas , Nepal is just like an imagination.

Nepal is sovereign and independent country and being a small and landlock country it is multi-cultural, multi-lingual, multi-regional, multi-racial country with 125 ethnic groups , 123 languages and more than 8 religions.
